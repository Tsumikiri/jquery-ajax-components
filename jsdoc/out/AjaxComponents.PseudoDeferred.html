Class: PseudoDeferred

PseudoDeferred

A dumbed-down Promise implementation. The main difference with a true promise is that PseudoDeferred objects can resolve and reject more than once. Also, callbacks added after a resolve or reject are not called until the next resolution or rejection.

Adds an always callback to the instance. Multiple callbacks can be added in this way (one at a time). Every always callback will be executed when the instance is resolved or rejected.
